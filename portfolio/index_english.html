BUT GEII

The University Bachelor of Technology in GEII is a multidisciplinary program that combines electrical engineering, electronics, industrial computing, and automation. This three-year curriculum trains professionals capable of designing, developing, and maintaining intelligent systems in complex technical environments.

Thanks to a balanced approach between theoretical instruction, practical work, and projects, students acquire concrete and versatile technical expertise, closely aligned with the needs of the industrial sector.

Graduates can quickly enter the workforce or continue their studies. They have access to a wide range of careers such as senior technician, automation engineer, embedded systems developer, or technical project manager, in fields such as energy, robotics, smart networks, or Industry 4.0.

The GEII program thus trains key players in tomorrow’s industrial innovation.
